Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2025 · Effective immediately

Short version: We collect only what's needed to generate your deck and process your payment. We don't sell your data. We don't share your startup information with anyone.

Who we are

DeckAI is an AI-powered pitch deck generator built for Indian startup founders. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at this website.

What information we collect

When you use DeckAI, we collect:

  • Startup information you provide — your startup name, industry, problem description, solution, market size, traction, team details, and funding ask. This is used solely to generate your pitch deck.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Razorpay. We do not store your card numbers, UPI handles, or banking details. We only receive a payment confirmation and order ID from Razorpay.
  • Basic usage data — page visits and error logs to maintain service quality. We do not use third-party tracking pixels or behavioural ad networks.

How we use your information

  • To generate your pitch deck, one-pager, and PDF files
  • To verify your payment and allow file downloads
  • To diagnose technical errors if generation fails
  • To improve our AI prompts and slide quality over time (in aggregate, never individually identifiable)

How long we retain your data

Your startup form data and generated files are stored in memory for up to 24 hours after generation, solely to allow you to download your files. After 24 hours, session data is automatically cleared. We do not maintain a long-term database of your startup information.

Who we share data with

We share data with two third parties, only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing. Razorpay's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.
  • Anthropic (AI provider) — your startup details are sent to an AI API to generate slide content. Anthropic's data handling policies apply to API usage. Your data is not used to train their models under standard API usage terms.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

Cookies

We use only a session cookie to maintain your current order state during a single visit. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or persistent identifiers.

Your rights

Since we do not maintain a long-term database of user data, there is limited personal data to access or delete. If you have questions about data we may hold, contact us at the email below and we will respond within 7 business days.

Security

All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Payment processing uses Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure. We do not store payment card data at any point.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. We will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of DeckAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at: hello@deckai.in
